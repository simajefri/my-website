# Personal Academic Website Specification
## Sima Jafarikhah - University of North Carolina Wilmington

### Project Overview
A professional yet personal academic website that showcases Dr. Sima Jafarikhah's research, teaching, and personal interests. The site will balance academic achievements with personal touches, creating an engaging and approachable online presence.

### Target Audience
- Academic colleagues and researchers
- Students and potential collaborators
- Conference organizers and journal editors
- General public interested in cybersecurity

### Design Theme
- **Style**: Professional and clean with warmer tones
- **Aesthetic**: Minimalist and modern
- **Color Palette**: Professional blues/greens with warmer accent colors
- **Typography**: Modern and approachable while maintaining academic credibility

### Website Structure

#### 1. Home/About Page (Main Landing Page)
**Content:**
- Name: Dr. Sima Jafarikhah
- Title: Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science
- Institution: University of North Carolina Wilmington
- Professional photo (filename: pic)
- Concise professional summary highlighting double doctorate in Mathematics and Computer Science
- Main research areas (3 key areas)
- Contact information integrated into the page
- LinkedIn profile button/link

**Layout:**
- Hero section with name, title, and photo (pic)
- Brief professional summary (2-3 sentences)
- Research highlights section
- Contact information section with LinkedIn button
- Navigation menu

**Content:**

  - Cybersecurity (scam, fraud and social engineering defense)

**Layout:**
- Research areas as featured sections
- Current projects showcase
- Publications summary with prominent Google Scholar link
- Grants and awards section

#### 3. Teaching Page
**Content:**

  - Discrete Mathematics
