I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at University of North Carolina Wilmington. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics and Computer Science. My research interest lies in a spectrum of topics in security and privacy, including but not limited to cybersecurity defense (scam, fraud and social engineering defense), cryptography protocol design for applications, and the application of AI in security.
